# The Upended Product Cycle

Building is now faster than speccing. What does that mean for product managers?

👉 **[View Presentation](https://kunalbabre.github.io/pm-builder/)**

## The World Changed

The economics of product development have flipped. Specs that took weeks now take hours. Sprints compress to a day. Signals from LinkedIn killing their APM program, Shopify mandating AI-first exploration, 25% of YC startups being 95% AI-built, and Andrew Ng proposing 2 PMs per engineer all point the same way: the PM role is expanding, not shrinking.

When the cost of building drops to near zero, the cost of *not* building becomes enormous.

## Product Taste

When AI can build anything, knowing *what* to build is the superpower. Taste breaks down into four forces:

- **Point of View**: A strong opinion that differentiates. Nobody is neutral on the Cybertruck.
- **Craft**: Obsessive care for detail. The opposite of AI slop.
- **Empathy**: Solve the emotional need, not just the functional one.
- **Delight**: The unexpected moments users love.

## 5 PM Beliefs Upended

1. **"Front-load all research before building"** → Build to think. The loop is hours, not weeks.
2. **"Think in documents"** → Think in prototypes. Show, don't tell.
3. **"Say no to most features"** → Say no to bad problems, not experiments. When validation is cheap, run both.
4. **"AI is a tool you use"** → Capture patterns. Invest in context. It compounds.
5. **"Data-driven decisions"** → Lead with judgment, verify with data.

## The PM Builder Stack

A 5-level framework. Start wherever you are:

| Level | Focus | Timeline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| 1 | **Use AI Daily**: Meetings, drafts, feedback, codebase exploration | Start today |
| 2 | **Build Context**: Product principles, instruction files, decision logs | Start this week |
| 3 | **Build Prototypes**: Validate before sprint backlog | Start this month |
| 4 | **Ship Products**: Co-author with AI, open PRs | For hands-on PMs |
| 5 | **Build the Culture**: Coach PMs, reward learning | Leadership multiplier |

Everyone should reach Level 3 within a month.

## The Thesis

Every PM is a Product Builder. The only question is whether you'll build.
